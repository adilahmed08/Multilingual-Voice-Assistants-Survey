# Multilingual-Voice-Assistants-Survey
Research summary on AI techniques for multilingual voice assistants in intelligent vehicles.

This research summary surveys Artificial Intelligence techniques used to build multilingual voice assistants for intelligent vehicles.

## Overview

Modern vehicles increasingly rely on voice-based human–machine interfaces (HMIs).  
This work analyses the AI technologies enabling multilingual voice interaction in automotive systems.

The research reviews techniques such as:

- Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)
- Natural Language Understanding (NLU)
- Dialogue Management
- Text-to-Speech (TTS)
- Machine Translation
- Large Language Models (LLMs)

It also examines real-world challenges - including accent variation, code-switching, cultural differences, and real-time constraints in in-vehicle systems.

## Topics Covered

- Multilingual speech recognition
- Cross-lingual NLP models
- Dialogue management for voice assistants
- Automotive human–machine interaction
- Evaluation metrics for speech AI systems
